About Me

Welcome to my website, That Awkward Artist. My name is Anni Sweeney and come from Donegal, Ireland. Here you will find all my latest artwork and is the first time I am showcasing them on the world wide web. The 3 mediums I mostly use are oil, pencil and biro. As you will see, when painting, I'm mostly inspired to paint my favourite musicians and actors
If you are interested in me painting something for you, please complete the contact form as the bottom of this page and I will get in touch with you.

Oil Painting

For my oil paintings, I use Georgian Oil which is traditionally crafted and triple-milled in England, Georgian Oil colours provide colour consistency from wet to dry and an even performance and finish across all colours. With a smooth and buttery consistency, Georgian Oil colours are ideal for experimenting with different brush techniques.

Biro Drawing

Ballpoint pen artwork is low cost and portable. In my spare time, I would draw my favourite musicians on post-it notes and coffee cups to keep myself amused. Before any long bus journey, I'd grab a coffee before getting the bus, I would start to decorate the cup.
